Tisza Lab

Hypothesis-driven Microbial Genomics

Baylor College of Medicine · Houston, TX

Scientists' ability to conduct genomic sequencing on microbes and communities of microbes has outpaced the ability to understand the meaning of those sequences. We build powerful computational tools that generate testable predictions and validate them in the laboratory.

Research Focus Areas

Viral Pathogen Genomics

From swabs to sewage — understanding viral evolution, detection, and surveillance in clinical and environmental samples.

Bacterial Epigenomics

Understanding the diversity and functional impact of DNA modification systems across bacterial genomes.

Virus Discovery

Discovering, annotating, and characterizing novel viruses in and on the human body using metagenomics.
